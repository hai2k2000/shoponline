# Product Context

## Users

- Store owner/admin.
- Manager.
- Sales staff.
- Warehouse staff.
- Accountant.
- Marketing staff.
- Public customers buying products online.

## Role Intent

- `ADMIN`: full system access.
- `MANAGER`: monitor and control operations.
- `SALES`: manage customers and orders.
- `WAREHOUSE`: manage products and stock.
- `ACCOUNTANT`: manage expenses, debts, revenue, and finance reports.
- `MARKETING`: manage content and marketing reporting.

## Public Customer Flow

1. Visit home page.
2. Browse categories or product listing.
3. View product detail.
4. Add to cart or buy now.
5. Checkout with customer information and payment method.
6. Track order by order code and phone number.

## Admin Flow

1. Login.
2. Review dashboard.
3. Manage catalog and inventory.
4. Process orders.
5. Track customers and suppliers.
6. Record expenses and debts.
7. Review reports.
8. Manage users/settings.

## Product Principles

- Real workflows over mock screens.
- Data consistency is more important than visual shortcuts.
- List-first admin pages with modal/drawer create/edit/detail where appropriate.
- All important actions should be traceable through `ActivityLog`.

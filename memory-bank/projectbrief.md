# Project Brief

## Name

Business Hub / ShopOnline

## Purpose

Build a real ecommerce and business operations platform that supports public online sales and internal admin operations.

## Core Outcomes

- Sell products online through a public storefront.
- Manage products, categories, product images, pricing, SEO, and promotions.
- Manage inventory with import, export, adjustment, history, and low-stock alerts.
- Manage customers, customer value, purchase history, notes, and segmentation.
- Manage orders from creation to fulfillment, cancellation, and return.
- Manage suppliers, purchase history, and debt tracking.
- Manage finance: revenue, expenses, debts, and profit.
- Provide dashboards and business reports based on real database values.
- Provide role based access control.
- Prepare for automation and AI expansion after the core system is stable.

## Acceptance Criteria

Demo must include:

- Product CRUD.
- Category CRUD.
- Inventory CRUD.
- Customer CRUD.
- Order CRUD.
- Expense CRUD.
- Dashboard.
- Reports.
- Authentication.
- Permissions.

## Non-Goals For Initial Phase

- Do not start marketplace integrations before core workflows are stable.
- Do not start AI features before reliable reporting data exists.
- Do not build static demo-only dashboards.
